BCI BreastCancerTStageCS CodeSystem

Tumor category according to AJCC breast cancer staging recommendations.

This code system http://hl7.org/fhir/us/bci/oncology/cs/BreastCancerTStageCS defines the following codes:

CodeDisplayDefinition
TX The primary tumor cannot be evaluated.The primary tumor cannot be evaluated.
Tis_DCIS DCIS is a noninvasive cancer, but if not removed it can develop into an invasive breast cancer later. DCIS means that cancer cells have been found in breast ducts and have not spread past the layer of tissue where they began.DCIS is a noninvasive cancer, but if not removed it can develop into an invasive breast cancer later. DCIS means that cancer cells have been found in breast ducts and have not spread past the layer of tissue where they began.
Tis_Paget Paget’s disease of the nipple is a rare form of early, noninvasive cancer that is only in the skin cells of the nipple. Sometimes Paget’s disease is associated with another, invasive breast cancer. If there is also an invasive breast cancer present, it is classified according to the stage of the invasive tumor.Paget’s disease of the nipple is a rare form of early, noninvasive cancer that is only in the skin cells of the nipple.
