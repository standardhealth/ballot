The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/bci/StructureDefinition/oncology-BreastCancer

FindingTopic or diagnosis of cancer originating in the tissues of the breast, and potentially spread to other organs of the body.

This profile builds on US Core Condition (a.k.a Problem) Profile.
